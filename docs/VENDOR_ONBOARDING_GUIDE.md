## Getting Started

### System Requirements
- **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest version)
- **Internet**: Stable connection (minimum 5 Mbps recommended)
- **Device**: Desktop, laptop, or tablet (mobile browser supported)

### Create Your Account

1. **Visit the Registration Page**
   - Go to [https://stores.stepperslife.com/register](https://stores.stepperslife.com/register)
   - Fill in your details:
     - Full Name
     - Email Address
     - Password (minimum 8 characters)

2. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for a verification email
   - Click the verification link
   - Your account is now active!

3. **First Login**
   - Go to [https://stores.stepperslife.com/login](https://stores.stepperslife.com/login)
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Sign In"

## Creating Your Store

### Step 1: Access Store Creation

After logging in for the first time:
1. You'll be redirected to the "Create Store" page automatically
2. Or navigate to [https://stores.stepperslife.com/create-store](https://stores.stepperslife.com/create-store)

### Step 2: Fill in Store Details

#### Required Information:

**1. Store Name**
- Choose a memorable name for your store
- Example: "Steppers Paradise", "Dance Elegance"
- **Tip**: Make it unique and related to stepping/dancing

**2. Store URL (Slug)**
- This will be your store's unique web address
- Format: `stores.stepperslife.com/store/your-store-name`
- Example: `steppers-paradise`
- **Rules**:
  - Lowercase letters only
  - Use hyphens (-) instead of spaces
  - No special characters
  - Must be unique

**3. Tagline (Optional)**
- A short catchy phrase describing your store
- Example: "Your one-stop shop for stepping gear"
- Maximum 100 characters

**4. Description**
- Detailed description of your store and products
- Tell customers what makes your store special
- Include information about:
  - Types of products you sell
  - Your experience in the industry
  - Quality and service commitments
- Minimum 50 characters, maximum 1000 characters

**5. Contact Information**
- **Email**: Customer service email (visible to customers)
- **Phone** (Optional): Contact number for customer inquiries

**6. Store Logo** (Optional)
- Upload a square image (recommended 500Ã—500px)
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- **Tip**: Use a high-quality image with your brand colors

#### Optional Information:

**7. Social Media Links**
- Facebook page URL
- Instagram profile URL
- Twitter handle

**8. Store Policies** (Can be added later)
- Return policy
- Shipping policy
- Privacy policy

### Step 3: Submit Your Store

1. Review all information carefully
2. Click "Create Store" button
3. Your store will be created instantly!
4. You'll be redirected to your vendor dashboard

## Adding Products

### Navigate to Products Section

1. Log in to your vendor dashboard
2. Click "Products" in the sidebar
3. Click "Add New Product" button

### Product Information Form

#### Basic Details:

**1. Product Name** (Required)
- Clear, descriptive name
- Example: "Premium Stepping Shoes - Black"
- Maximum 200 characters

**2. Product Description** (Required)
- Detailed description of the product
- Include:
  - Features and benefits
  - Materials used
  - Care instructions
  - Size guide (if applicable)
- Minimum 50 characters, maximum 2000 characters
- **Tip**: Use bullet points for easy reading

**3. Category** (Required)
Select from:
- Shoes
- Clothing
- Accessories
- Merchandise
- Other

**4. SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)** (Optional)
- Your internal product code
- Example: "STEP-SHOE-BLK-001"
- Maximum 100 characters

#### Pricing:

**5. Price** (Required)
- Your product price in USD ($)
- Example: $129.99
- **Note**: Platform fee (7%) will be deducted from each sale

**6. Compare At Price** (Optional)
- Original price before discount
- Used to show savings
- Example: Price $99.99, Compare At $149.99
- **Shows**: "Save $50!"

#### Inventory:

**7. Quantity** (Required)
- Number of items in stock
- Example: 25
- **Tip**: Keep this updated to avoid overselling

**8. Track Inventory**
- âœ… Enable: System tracks stock automatically
- âŒ Disable: Unlimited stock (for digital products or services)
- **Recommended**: Enable for physical products

**9. Low Stock Threshold** (Optional)
- Get notified when stock is low
- Example: Set to 5 to get alert when 5 items left
- Default: 5 items

#### Images:

**10. Product Images** (Required - minimum 1)
- Upload 1-10 high-quality images
- **Requirements**:
  - Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
  - Recommended size: 1200Ã—1200px or larger
  - Maximum file size: 5MB per image
  - Square format recommended
- **Optimization**: Images automatically optimized to WebP format
- **Sizes generated**:
  - Thumbnail (150Ã—150px)
  - Small (300Ã—300px)
  - Medium (600Ã—600px)
  - Large (1200Ã—1200px)

**Image Tips**:
- Use white or neutral backgrounds
- Show product from multiple angles
- Include close-up details
- Show product in use (if applicable)
- First image is the main product image

#### Product Status:

**11. Status**
- **Active**: Product visible to customers
- **Draft**: Product hidden, work in progress
- **Archived**: Product hidden but kept in system

#### Variants (Advanced):

**12. Product Variants** (Optional)
- Add variations like sizes, colors, etc.
- Example variants:
  - Size: Small, Medium, Large, XL
  - Color: Black, White, Red, Blue
  - Material: Leather, Suede, Canvas

**To Add Variants**:
1. Click "Add Variant" button
2. Enter variant name (e.g., "Size: Large")
3. Set variant-specific price (if different)
4. Set variant-specific stock quantity
5. Click "Save Variant"

### Step 4: Save Product

1. Review all information
2. Click "Save Product" button
3. Product is now added to your store!

**Result**:
- **Active products**: Visible immediately to customers
- **Draft products**: Only visible to you in dashboard

### View Orders

**Order List Columns**:
- **Order Number**: Unique order ID
- **Customer**: Customer name
- **Status**: Current order status
- **Total**: Order total amount
- **Date**: Order placement date
- **Actions**: View details, fulfill order

### Order Statuses

| Status | Meaning | Your Action |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Pending** | Payment processing | Wait for confirmation |
| **Paid** | Payment confirmed | Prepare shipment |
| **Fulfilled** | Order shipped | Track shipment |
| **Delivered** | Customer received | None (order complete) |
| **Cancelled** | Order cancelled | Process refund if needed |
| **Refunded** | Payment returned | None |

### Fulfill an Order

When an order is **Paid**, you can fulfill it:

1. **Navigate to Order**
   - Dashboard â†’ Orders
   - Click on order to view details

2. **Prepare Shipment**
   - Pack items securely
   - Print shipping label
   - Include packing slip (optional)

3. **Mark as Fulfilled**
   - Enter tracking information:
     - Shipping carrier (USPS, UPS, FedEx, etc.)
     - Tracking number
   - Optional: Notification message to customer
   - Click "Mark as Fulfilled" button

4. **Automatic Notifications**
   - âœ… Customer receives email with tracking info
   - âœ… Status updates to "Fulfilled"
   - âœ… Customer can track shipment

### Cancel an Order

**Before Fulfillment**:
1. Navigate to order details
2. Click "Cancel Order" button
3. Select cancellation reason
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Refund processes automatically

**After Fulfillment**:

- Check customs requirements
- Include customs declaration
- Add insurance for valuable items

## Payment & Fees

### Platform Fees

**Commission Rate**: 7% per sale

**Example Calculation**:
```
Product Price: $100.00
Platform Fee (7%): -$7.00
Your Earnings: $93.00
```

**Fee Breakdown**:
- Payment processing (Stripe): ~2.9% + $0.30
- Platform commission: 7%
- **Total fees**: ~10% + $0.30 per transaction

### Payment Schedule

**Payout Timing**:
- Automatic payouts every 2 weeks
- First payout: 14 days after first sale
- Subsequent payouts: Every other Friday

**Minimum Payout**: $25.00

**Payment Method**:
- Direct deposit (ACH) - US banks
- Bank transfer - International banks
- **Setup**: Dashboard â†’ Settings â†’ Payments

### Payment Setup

1. **Connect Stripe Account**
   - Dashboard â†’ Settings â†’ Payments
   - Click "Connect Stripe Account"
   - Follow Stripe onboarding process
   - Provide:
     - Business information
     - Bank account details
     - Tax identification number

2. **Verification**
   - Stripe may request additional documents
   - Typical verification time: 1-3 business days
   - You'll receive email when approved

3. **Start Receiving Payments**
   - Once verified, you can accept orders
   - Funds automatically deposited to bank account

### Tax Reporting

**1099-K Forms**:
- If you earn > $600/year, you'll receive 1099-K
- Sent by January 31st each year
- Available in dashboard (Coming soon)

**Sales Tax**:
- Automatically calculated based on customer location
- Collected at checkout
- Remitted to you with each sale
- **Your responsibility**: File and remit sales tax to states

**Tax Resources**:
- Consult with tax professional
- Use tax software (TaxJar, Avalara)
- Learn more: [IRS Small Business Tax](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Issue 1: Can't Upload Product Images

**Symptoms**:
- Error message when uploading
- Image won't appear after upload

**Solutions**:
1. **Check file size**: Maximum 5MB per image
   - Compress images using [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com)
2. **Check format**: Use JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - Convert other formats using image editor
3. **Clear browser cache**: Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
4. **Try different browser**: Switch to Chrome or Firefox

#### Issue 2: Orders Not Appearing

**Symptoms**:
- Can't see recent orders
- Order count doesn't match

**Solutions**:
1. **Refresh page**: Press F5 or Ctrl+R
2. **Check filter**: Ensure "All Orders" filter is selected
3. **Clear cache**: Hard refresh with Ctrl+Shift+R
4. **Contact support**: If persists after 1 hour

#### Issue 3: Product Not Visible to Customers

**Symptoms**:
- Product saved but not showing on storefront

**Solutions**:
1. **Check status**: Product must be "Active"
   - Dashboard â†’ Products â†’ Edit Product â†’ Status â†’ Active
2. **Check inventory**: Quantity must be > 0 (if tracking inventory)
3. **Clear browser cache**: Force refresh
4. **Wait 5 minutes**: Cache may need to expire

#### Issue 4: Payment/Payout Issues

**Symptoms**:
- Payout delayed or missing
- Bank account not receiving funds

**Solutions**:
1. **Verify Stripe connection**: Dashboard â†’ Settings â†’ Payments
2. **Check bank details**: Ensure correct account info
3. **Minimum threshold**: Must have at least $25.00 to payout
4. **Timing**: Payouts occur every 2 weeks (Friday)
5. **Contact Stripe support**: For account-specific issues

#### Issue 5: Forgot Password

**Solutions**:
1. Go to [login page](https://stores.stepperslife.com/login)
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check inbox for reset link
5. Click link and set new password

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How much does it cost to open a store?**
A: Opening a store is **FREE**! We only charge a 7% commission on sales.

**Q: Is there a monthly subscription fee?**
A: No! Stepperslife Shop has no monthly fees. You only pay when you make sales.

**Q: Can I sell on other platforms too?**
A: Yes! You're free to sell on other platforms simultaneously.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Payments are automatically deposited to your bank account every 2 weeks via Stripe.

**Q: What if I need help?**
A: Contact us at support@stepperslife.com or use the "Help" button in your dashboard.

### Product Questions

**Q: How many products can I list?**
A: Unlimited! List as many products as you want.

**Q: Can I edit products after listing?**
A: Yes! Edit products anytime from Dashboard â†’ Products â†’ Edit.

**Q: What product categories are available?**
A: Shoes, Clothing, Accessories, Merchandise, and Other.

**Q: Can I sell digital products?**
A: Not yet. Currently only physical products are supported. Digital products coming soon!

**Q: Do you handle inventory for me?**
A: No, you're responsible for managing your own inventory and shipping.

### Order Questions

**Q: What happens when someone places an order?**
A: You receive an email notification and can view the order in your dashboard.

**Q: Do I ship directly to customers?**
A: Yes, you handle all shipping and fulfillment.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Yes, you can cancel orders before fulfillment. Contact support for post-fulfillment cancellations.

**Q: Who handles customer service?**
A: You're responsible for customer service for your products. Platform support handles account/technical issues.

### Payment Questions

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Automatic payouts every 2 weeks (every other Friday).

**Q: What's the minimum payout amount?**
A: $25.00

**Q: How do platform fees work?**
A: We charge 7% commission on each sale. Stripe payment processing (~2.9% + $0.30) is additional.

**Q: Do I need a business license?**
A: Check your local regulations. Some states/cities require business licenses for selling.

**Q: How do I handle sales tax?**

**Q: Is my data secure?**
