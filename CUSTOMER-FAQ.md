## üõçÔ∏è Shopping & Ordering

### How do I place an order?

1. Browse products at [stores.stepperslife.com](https://stores.stepperslife.com)
2. Click on a product to view details
3. Select size/variant (if applicable)
4. Click **"Add to Cart"**
5. Click cart icon to review your items
6. Click **"Checkout"**
7. Enter shipping information
8. Select payment method
9. Complete payment
10. You'll receive an order confirmation email!

### Can I modify my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement. After that, the vendor may have already started processing your order. Contact the vendor directly through your order page to request changes.

### How do I track my order?

1. Check your email for shipping confirmation
2. Click the tracking link in the email, or
3. Visit your order history (if you have an account)
4. Enter your tracking number on the carrier's website

### Can I cancel my order?

Yes, if the order hasn't shipped yet:
1. Contact the vendor immediately through your order page
2. Vendors typically respond within 24 hours
3. Once approved, refunds process within 5-7 business days

## üí≥ Payment & Pricing

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods depending on the vendor:
- **Credit/Debit Cards** (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- **Apple Pay** & **Google Pay**
- **PayPal**
- **Cash** (for local pickup only)

### Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and never store your full credit card details. All payments are processed through PCI-compliant processors (Stripe, PayPal, Square).

### Why was I charged a different amount than the product price?

Your total includes:
- Product price
- Sales tax (varies by state)
- Shipping cost (if applicable)

All charges are shown in your cart before you complete checkout.

### Can I use a discount code?

Yes! Enter your discount code at checkout before completing payment. Discount codes are provided by individual vendors or through promotional campaigns.

### Do you charge sales tax?

Yes, applicable sales tax is calculated based on your shipping address and local tax rates.

## üì¶ Shipping & Delivery

### How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs vary by:
- Vendor location
- Your delivery address
- Package weight
- Shipping speed selected

Exact shipping cost is calculated at checkout before you pay.

### How long does shipping take?

**Standard Shipping:** 5-7 business days
**Expedited Shipping:** 2-3 business days
**Express Shipping:** 1-2 business days

Note: Processing time (1-2 business days) is additional.

### Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the United States. International shipping is planned for future release.

### What if my package is lost or damaged?

Contact the vendor immediately through your order page with:
- Order number
- Photos of damage (if applicable)
- Description of the issue

Vendors will work with you to resolve the issue through replacement or refund.

### Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

Contact the vendor immediately if the order hasn't shipped. Once shipped, address changes must be made through the shipping carrier.

## üîÑ Returns & Refunds

### What is your return policy?

Return policies vary by vendor. Check the individual vendor's return policy on their store page or product listing.

**Standard policy guidelines:**
- **Return window:** 30 days from delivery
- **Condition:** Items must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging
- **Proof of purchase:** Original order number required

### How do I return an item?

1. Contact the vendor through your order page
2. Explain reason for return
3. Vendor will provide return instructions
4. Ship item back (you may be responsible for return shipping)
5. Refund processed once vendor receives the return

### When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within:
- **5-7 business days** after vendor receives the return
- **Additional 3-5 days** for funds to appear in your account

Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

### Can I exchange an item?

Yes! Contact the vendor to request an exchange. Availability depends on the vendor's inventory.

### What items cannot be returned?

Common non-returnable items:
- Custom or personalized items
- Final sale items
- Underwear or intimate apparel (for hygiene reasons)
- Digital downloads

Check the specific vendor's policy for details.

## üë§ Account & Profile

### Do I need an account to shop?

No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account offers benefits:
- Order history tracking
- Saved addresses
- Faster checkout
- Order updates
- Easy returns

### How do I create an account?

1. Click **"Sign In"** at the top of the page
2. Click **"Create Account"**
3. Use your SteppersLife account (SSO) to sign in
4. Complete your profile

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Since we use SteppersLife SSO (Single Sign-On):
1. Go to [stepperslife.com](https://stepperslife.com)
2. Click **"Forgot Password"**
3. Follow password reset instructions
4. Use new password to log into stores

### How do I update my shipping address?

1. Sign in to your account
2. Go to **"Account Settings"**
3. Click **"Addresses"**
4. Add or edit addresses

### Can I delete my account?

Yes. Contact support@stepperslife.com to request account deletion. Note: Order history will be retained for accounting purposes.

## üîß Technical Issues

### The website isn't loading. What should I do?

1. Refresh your browser (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. If issues persist, contact support@stepperslife.com

### Why can't I add items to my cart?

Common solutions:
- The item may be out of stock
- Clear your browser cache
- Disable browser extensions
- Try a different browser
- Make sure JavaScript is enabled

### I'm having trouble checking out. Help!

1. Verify all required fields are filled out
2. Check that your payment information is correct
3. Try a different payment method
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try a different browser
6. Contact support@stepperslife.com with your order details

### Why is my tracking number not working?

- Tracking numbers take 24-48 hours to activate in carrier systems
- Check that you're using the correct carrier's website
- Verify the tracking number is correct
- Contact the vendor if tracking hasn't updated after 48 hours
